History of this clinic

Our eye clinic has a rich history rooted in a commitment to excellence in eye care. Founded over 5 years ago by Srinivasan N, our clinic has been a pillar of the community, providing compassionate and cutting-edge eye care services to patients of all ages.

Over the years, we have built a reputation for delivering personalized treatment plans tailored to each patient's unique needs, whether they require routine eye exams, advanced surgical interventions, or specialized treatments for various eye conditions. Our team of experienced ophthalmologists, optometrists, and support staff are dedicated to upholding the highest standards of care and continually strive to stay at the forefront of advancements in eye health technology and practices.

As we look to the future, we remain committed to our founding principles of excellence, compassion, and innovation, ensuring that our patients receive the best possible eye care experience for years to come.

In addition to providing exceptional eye care services, our clinic has also been actively involved in community outreach programs aimed at promoting eye health and awareness. We regularly conduct free eye screenings, educational seminars, and awareness campaigns to reach underserved populations and raise awareness about the importance of regular eye exams and preventive measures.

Furthermore, our clinic has established strong partnerships with local hospitals, medical institutions, and charitable organizations to expand access to eye care services and facilitate collaborative research initiatives. Through these partnerships, we strive to advance the field of ophthalmology, improve patient outcomes, and contribute to the global effort to combat preventable blindness and vision impairment.
